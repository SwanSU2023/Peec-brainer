## Module 3 вҖ” Structural Audit (the differentiator)

**Why the brand page isn't cited, at two structural layers.**

Most competitors in this challenge stop at "here are the pages with low visibility." Peec Brain goes one step deeper and diagnoses the *structural* reason the brand page is ignored, along two axes:

**Layer A вҖ” Editorial structure.** Parses Peec `get_url_content` markdown for: H2/H3 density, named active ingredients, expert quote presence (MD, FAAD, DO), comparison tables, per-product standardised sections, TOC/anchors, `How we tested` and `Meet the experts` signals.

**Layer B вҖ” Technical structure (schema.org).** Parses raw HTML with `extruct` for JSON-LD, microdata, RDFa, Open Graph. Scores presence of `Product`, `AggregateRating`, `Review`, `FAQPage`, `HowTo`, `BreadcrumbList`, `Article`, `Organization`.

Diff = signals present on a majority of LLM-cited pages but absent on the brand page вҶ’ prescriptive actions.

### Real run вҖ” LancГҙme on *"Quel est le meilleur sГ©rum anti-Гўge en 2026 selon les dermatologues ?"*

LancГҙme's AI visibility on this prompt: **0 %**. ChatGPT cites 8 competitors and 15 source domains in its place.

| URL | citation_rate | H2 | ingredients | experts | table | JSON-LD types |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `lancome.fr/soin/par-categorie/serum-visage` | **0.00** | **0** | **0** | **0** | **вҖ”** | `BreadcrumbList, ListItem, Organization, WebPage` |
| `vogue.fr/beaute/article/meilleurs-serums-anti-age` | 0.36 | 11 | 12 | 1 | вҖ” | `AggregateRating, Article, Product, Review, Rating, Person` + 3 more |
| `healthline.com/.../best-anti-aging-serum` | **1.00** | 8 | 17 | 2 | вң“ | `FAQPage, ItemList, Product, Question, Answer` + 7 more |
| `vogue.co.uk/article/best-anti-ageing-serums` | 0.88 | 11 | 12 | 2 | вҖ” | `AggregateRating, Article, Product, Review, Rating, Person` + 3 more |

**Verdict generated by Peec Brain**: *"Gaps structurels majeurs dГ©tectГ©s : 3 Г©ditoriaux, 4 schema. Les LLMs ne trouvent pas le signal attendu sur la page marque вҖ” ils se rabattent sur les 3 pages Г©ditoriales citГ©es."*

**Prescriptive actions** (7 emitted, excerpt):
1. Ajouter au moins 4 sections H2 substantielles sur la page cible.
2. Nommer explicitement les ingrГ©dients actifs : acide hyaluronique, ascorbate, ceramide, collagen, retinol, peptidesвҖҰ
3. Inclure 1-2 citations de dermatologues experts (MD/FAAD explicitement visible).
4. ImplГ©menter JSON-LD `Product` sur les pages produit/catГ©gorie.
5. Exposer `AggregateRating` (note + nombre d'avis).
6. IntГ©grer `Review` JSON-LD pour les avis produits.
7. Restructurer la page comme `Article` avec auteur + dateModified + reviewedBy.

## Module 4 вҖ” Citation Authority Audit

**Who decided not to talk about you, and who they cite in your place.**

The content-gap stack of the challenge is crowded (Ritwika, Lukas, Drift Radar all ship variants of "pages with low visibility"). Nobody ships this: the domain-level mirror of the same gap вҖ” the editorial sources whose writers trust your competitors but have never sent a journalist to your brand.

### Pipeline

1. Peec `get_domain_report` filtered by prompt_id вҶ’ cited domains + `mentioned_brand_ids[]` + Peec classification (EDITORIAL / UGC / CORPORATE / вҖҰ).
2. Build **gap list** = domains citing at least one tracked competitor but never the own brand.
3. Enrich with Ahrefs `site-explorer-domain-rating` вҶ’ DR + Ahrefs rank per domain.
4. Classify domain typology via curated whitelist + Peec classification fallback: `editorial_premium` / `editorial_health` / `specialized` / `retailer` / `ugc` / `reference` / `corporate`.
5. Priority score = `(citation_count + retrieval_count Г— 0.3) Г— (DR/100) Г— typology_weight`.
6. Emit per-typology outreach action. Export CSV sorted by priority.

## Module 2 вҖ” Content Gap (traffic-first prioritization)

**Your page at 1 169 visites/mois that ChatGPT never cites. That one first.**

Several other submissions already ship content-gap analyzers (Ritwika's `workflow-1`, Lukas's `content-gap-hunt`, the Drift Radar "silence split"). Our angle: we don't just return the pages with 0% visibility, we **prioritize by existing business value** вҖ” `priority_score = Ahrefs top-page traffic Г— (1 вҲ’ AI visibility)`.

The signal: a page already earning 1 000+ monthly visits on Google, but invisible in LLMs, is where every euro of optimization work should go first.

### Real run вҖ” LancГҙme

**20 pages prioritized. Top gap:** `lancome.fr/beauty-magazine/la-routine-skincare-parfaite-pour-ma-peau.html` вҖ” 1 169 monthly organic visits on Google, **0 % AI visibility on 5 distinct Peec prompts**. Peec Brain ranks it before any low-traffic page and emits the exact optimization action.

Full output: [`outputs/lancome_content_gaps.csv`](outputs/lancome_content_gaps.csv)

Integrated Ahrefs enrichment: per-URL `sum_traffic`, `top_keyword`, `top_keyword_best_position`.

## Module 1 вҖ” Prompt Discovery (fusion GSC + Ahrefs + product catalog)

**The prompts you should be tracking вҖ” that you're not.**

Alexander Gumtz's `gsc2peecai` already occupies the pure GSC вҶ’ Peec axis with a dual-threshold archiving pattern and SISTRIX volumes. Our angle is a different one: we **fuse three SEO signals** вҖ” GSC impressions, Ahrefs `site-explorer-organic-keywords` (rank data, Alexander's tool doesn't have this), and the client's product catalog (specific product names вҶ’ specific prompts like *"La Vie Est Belle Intense avis"* that nobody else can reach from query data alone).

Pipeline:
1. Pull GSC top queries (when ownership available) + Ahrefs organic keywords.
2. Detect intent (branded / commercial / comparison / transactional / informational) via rule-based classifier with stopword filtering.
3. Match against a per-client `known_products` catalog вҶ’ route to specialized templates ("Que vaut le parfum X de LancГҙme en 2026 ?").
4. Deduplicate against existing Peec prompts (Jaccard on content tokens).
5. Propose new topics where categorization gaps exist.
6. Batch create via Peec `create_prompts` + `create_topics` (dry-run safe by default).

### Real run вҖ” LancГҙme

Starting state: 57 existing prompts, 1 topic ("LongГ©vitГ©"), 123 competitors tracked.

After Module 1 dry-run: **22 new prompts across 6 proposed topics** (Parfums, Maquillage Yeux, Maquillage Teint, Soins SГ©rums, Soins CrГЁmes, Soins Diagnostic). Top proposal consolidated from 7 keyword variants: *"Que vaut le parfum La Vie Est Belle de LancГҙme en 2026 ?"* with combined traffic 19 096/mo.

Full output: [`outputs/lancome_m1_dry_run.csv`](outputs/lancome_m1_dry_run.csv)
